This page is where I interview people working in the STEM industry to provide curious students and minorities with exposure to the opportunities available in the STEM field. From a young age, I was fortunate to be exposed to computer science and robotics, and those experiences helped me discover a passion for STEM. I know not everyone has that same early exposure, so I hope these interviews can help others discover their passions and see that there is a place for them in STEM. I hope this is a place where you can learn something new and be inspired. Check back soon! :)
