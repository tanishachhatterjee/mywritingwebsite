journey

This website is to showcase the growth of my writing.

where it started

My interest in writing sparked when the pandemic hit. I am a very talkative person (you can ask my family and friends), and I love sharing stories. Once school moved online, I wasn’t able to share stories with my friends like I used to, so I began writing just for fun! Soon, I started really enjoying writing because it was a way for me to relive my happy and funny memories.

where i've been

Then I started to write about issues I saw in my school because I realized I could use my voice to influence people to be better. Soon after, I started expanding those issues to the world, like air pollution. This allowed me to not only educate others about these problems but also educate myself. One of the articles I wrote was about a solution to air pollution: electric cars. This article sparked my passion for sustainable technology and technology that makes the world a better place environmentally. Researching and writing about new topics helps me to become curious about subjects that I otherwise would not have explored.

where i'm going

Now, I am expanding my writing by interviewing people working in the STEM industry and writing articles about their experiences, challenges, and accomplishments. The reason I am doing this is not only to grow as a writer by trying a new type of writing but also to help others discover their passions and see that there is a place for them in STEM. From a young age, I was fortunate to be exposed to computer science and robotics, and those experiences helped me discover a passion for STEM. I know not everyone has that same early exposure, so I hope my website can be a place where you can learn something new and be inspired.
